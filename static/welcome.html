How it works

  1. 1

    You drop in a video

    Upload a file or paste a YouTube link. Add optional context like "from my SXSW talk" to help the AI understand what it's watching.

  2. 2

    We transcribe it locally

    Your video is transcribed right on your Mac using Whisper. Nothing leaves your machine at this step.

  3. 3

    AI finds the best moments

    Claude reads the transcript and picks the most clip-worthy moments based on your voice strategy: the stories, insights, and hooks that represent you best.

  4. 4

    Clips get cut, posts get drafted

    Each moment becomes a video clip (formatted for LinkedIn or Instagram) with a publish-ready social post written in your voice.

  5. 5

    You review and publish

    Browse your clips in the Library, trim them if needed, copy the drafts, and post. You're always in control of the final result.
