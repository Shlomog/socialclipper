Your Voice Strategy

Using default

Let's figure out what makes your content yours. Your voice strategy tells us which moments to pick from your videos and how to frame your social posts. Without it, we're guessing. With it, every clip sounds like you.

Pick whichever path is easiest:

Option 1: Generate from your LinkedIn

Paste your LinkedIn headline, About section, and a few posts you're proud of. We'll analyze how you communicate and build a voice strategy that matches.

Option 2: Paste your own strategy

Already have a brand voice doc, content brief, or writing guidelines? Drop it right in. If you generated a strategy from LinkedIn above, it'll appear here for you to review and tweak before saving.

Option 3: Use any AI to help you write one

Not sure where to start? Copy this prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI you like. It'll walk you through a short conversation about your brand, then output a strategy you can paste into Option 2 above.

I need to create a voice strategy for a social media clipping tool that analyzes my videos and picks the best moments to turn into social posts. Help me define my voice strategy by asking me about: 1. Who I am — my role, expertise, and background 2. My target audience — who I'm trying to reach 3. My key topics — the 3-5 themes I always want to talk about 4. My unique angle — what makes my perspective different 5. What to prioritize — the kinds of moments that represent me best 6. What to avoid — topics, tones, or framings that don't fit my brand After our conversation, output a concise voice strategy document (under 300 words) that I can paste into my tool. It should be written as direct instructions, like: "Prioritize moments where the speaker..." "Avoid content that..." "The audience cares about..."
